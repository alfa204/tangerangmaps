About TM

TangerangMaps Android App is an application directory Tangerang city public facilities are equipped with maps from Google Maps. This application displays information about the Tangerang, public facilities based on the categories can be selected and important phone numbers in Tangerang. This application is based on a directory site which is located in Tangerang city www.tangerangmaps.com. Expected future applications can be one of the references on public places in Tangerang that can be accessed anywhere and anytime.
